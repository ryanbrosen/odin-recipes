How To Make Crepes

Description

Here is a recipe that I compiled regarding how to make crepes.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Strawberries
  • Bananas

Steps

  1. Crack three eggs into a bowl
  2. Stir eggs until yolk is mixed
  3. Add 1/2 cup of flour
  4. Mix flour and yolk
  5. Add 1 cup of milk
  6. Stir ingredients
  7. Cook on stovetop
  8. Add strawberries and banans
